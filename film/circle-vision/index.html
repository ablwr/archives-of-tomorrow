Circle-Vision 360°

AKA
(None)
Era
1955–
Format
analog
Developed by
Disney
(Max) Capacity
1 (360°)
Size
35mm × 9
Perforations
4 x 9
Frame size picture area
0.866" × 0.630"
  • This format was nine 35mm cameras and fit a projection screen that wrapped a full 360° around the cinema
  • Disney had developed a precursor to this format called Circarama, which used eleven 16mm cameras to create a circle of vision
  • This format premiered at Disneyland on July 17, 1955
