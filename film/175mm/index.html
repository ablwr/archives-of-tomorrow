Fun facts
  • The first use of this format was with the Birtac, which was a combined projector and camera
  • This format could be created by cutting strips of 35mm film in half
  • Several formats were developed to use this size, eight different kinds of film stock: Birtac, Biokam, Hughes, Gaumont, Clou, Duoscope, Movette, and Pathé Rural
