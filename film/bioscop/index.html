Fun facts
  • This format used two loops of 54mm filmstrips with no side perforations during filming and 4 perforations during projection
  • This system worked by projecting one frame from each strip of film in rapid succession
  • Footage from this camera debuted in July 1895 and Skladanowsky was awarded a patent in November 1895
