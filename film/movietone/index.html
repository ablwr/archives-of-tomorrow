Fun facts
  • This format had its own specific aspect ratio to accomodate the additional printed soundtrack; other formats used Silent (1.33:1) or Academy (1.375:1) aspect ratios
  • This was one of four sound technologies in development during the 1920s; the others were DeForest Phonofilm, Warner Brothers' Vitaphone, and RCA Photophone
  • This format was used for newsreels and a handful of motion pictures
