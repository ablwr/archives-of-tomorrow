  • Audio stored on this format could be analog or digital; video could only be analog
  • In the UK, "LaserDisc" was used for discs that hold digital audio and "LaserVision" was used for discs with analog audio and video
  • This format's digital audio quality was better than anything else available to consumers at the time, but analog audio quality was inconsistent
