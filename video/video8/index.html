Fun facts
  • This format was initially launched by Kodak but Sony ended up dominating the market with its Handycam camcorder line
  • This format was the first 8mm video tape format; its later predecessors were Hi8 and Digital8
  • This format's appeal was around its smaller size, which made for much lighter camcorders than VHS or Betamax
  • Fun fact about all 8mm: there was no separate linear audio track; it all AFM, recorded in the video signal, from the jump
