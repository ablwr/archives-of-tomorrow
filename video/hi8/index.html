Hi8

AKA
8mm, high-band Video8
Era
1989–2007
Analog/digital
analog
Format
Cassette
Developed by
Sony
(Max) Capacity
2 hours
Size

  • The format was developed as an improvement to Video8, with greater bandwidth for the brightness levels, but no increase to color resolution
  • Hi8 decks are typically backwards compatible, which means they can also play the older Video8 format
  • Some professional Hi8 equipment could store additional digital stereo PCM sound on a special reserved track
