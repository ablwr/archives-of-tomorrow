Fun facts
  • While predecessor 8mm formats Video8 and Hi8 were analog, this format stored digital video
  • A Video8 or Hi8 tape can be recorded in digital mode, but a 2 hour tape could only store 1 hour of digital content
  • Digital8 decks could only record in DV but could still read Video8 and Hi8 tapes
