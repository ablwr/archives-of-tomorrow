Fun facts
  • This format was Sony's response to Panasonic's DVCPRO, both of which target the professional instead of consumer video market
  • Many high-end DVCAM players would convert unlocked audio to locked audio on playback
  • This format has tracks that are 50% wider than standard DV, which increased robustness and reliability
